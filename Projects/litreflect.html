VR/AR Literature Reflections - My take on current VR/AR literature

This page will document my own reflections on current VR/AR literature (both academic and industry). I'll sum up articles in much the same manner I would for an informal report directed at my management. Each mini-report will consist of three sections:

  1. The Main Ideas: The primary takeaways from the article
  2. Connections: How the article connects to the VR/AR industry at large
  3. Personal Reflection: My own personal thoughts on what the article's points mean to me.
  4. Follow Up sources: Secondary sources in the article that I want to follow up on.
  5. References: A citation of both the original articles and any other sources referred to in the report.
