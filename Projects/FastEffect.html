AR Presenter

This project produced "AR Presenter": a Hololens-based augmented reality application designed to facilitate the trade/tech show experience. At the highest level, the product mimics the paradigm of Powerpoint, in that it allows presenters to build a "presentation" out of individual components, which can then be presented to viewers in a controlled manner.
